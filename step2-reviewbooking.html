Luggage Allowance

One small carry on per person is allowed (10kg maximum), roughly the same size as a carry on bag on a commercial airline flight. Your luggage will be inspected for size and weight when you arrive to the ASCENT Lounge or point of departure. If you have excess or oversized baggage, a member of Team ASCENT reserves the right to disallow a bag or bags that are deemed too heavy or large. In this case, ASCENT will coordinate ground transport of the bag(s) to the traveler’s destination, at the user’s expense. If there is room, ASCENT will make an effort to place your item on later flight.

Weather Policy

FACTORS DETERMINING WEATHER DELAYS OR CANCELLATIONS

We take safety seriously at ASCENT. There are several factors in the decision of whether a flight will be cancelled or delayed due to weather. These factors include aircraft type, weather along flight path and thunderstorms. Ultimately, it is up to the pilot to make the call on flight safety.

DELAYS OR CANCELLATION DUE TO WEATHER

If your flight is delayed or cancelled due to weather or any other circumstance, you will be notified by ASCENT via email, text or phone. If you would like to cancel a flight, please see Ascent Flight Cancellation Policies.

Flight Cancellation Policy

ASCENT Scheduled Flights:

You will receive credit that can be used on future ASCENT flights for your fare if you cancel more than 48 hours prior to the scheduled departure time. You can cancel your ticket by sending an email to contact@ascent.flights or by calling ASCENT at (12) 345 6789.

In the event of a cancellation pursuant to these terms, ASCENT will credit your account with the amount charged for the cancelled seat that may be used for future travel for up to 12 months from the date such credit is used. ASCNET will not offer cash refunds for any cancellations. A cancellation made within 48 hours of flight time will result in a forfeiture of the full ticket fare.

If you Chartered a Flight:

Charters of an entire helicopter must be cancelled with the following notice greater than 48 hours in advance of scheduled departure time of the Flight. You can cancel on the by sending an email to contact@ascent.flights or by calling ASCENT at (12) 345 6789. In the event of a cancellation pursuant to these terms, ASCENT will credit your Account with the amount charged for the cancelled Flight that may be used for future travel for up to 12 months from the date such credit is issued. A cancellation made within the required cancellation periods will result in a forfeiture of the full Charter price.

Corporate Clients:

Clients with corporate accounts will follow the same cancellation policy as mentioned above. ASCENT credits will be credited to corporate accounts instead of individual accounts.
